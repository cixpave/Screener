Live quotes (free)

Keep all 503 S&P 500 prices updating constantly with a free API key — no brokerage account needed. Keys are stored only in this browser.

Free tiers are rate-limited (Finnhub ~60 quotes/min), so Pulse refreshes what's on your screen and what you hold first, then cycles the rest of the index continuously.

Brokerage — thinkorswim (Schwab)

Log in with your Schwab account and your portfolio tab shows your real positions. Your password is typed only on schwab.com; access tokens stay in this browser and the app secret stays on the server.

One-time setup: create an app at developer.schwab.com (Accounts & Trading + Market Data products), set its callback URL to https://<your-domain>/api/schwab/callback, then add SCHWAB_APP_KEY and SCHWAB_SECRET as environment variables in your Vercel project.
